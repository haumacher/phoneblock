Report numbers as SPAM or legitimate

Your messages help the entire PhoneBlock community to protect themselves from SPAM calls.

Report SPAM number

If you receive a SPAM call, you can report the number directly in the app. The more details you provide, the more helpful your report will be for other users.

Step 1: Report SPAM

Tap on the "Report SPAM" button for the corresponding call in the history

Step 2: Select category

Select the appropriate category from the list

Step 3: Comment (optional)

Optionally add a comment to help other users

Available SPAM categories

  • Advertising: Unsolicited advertising calls
  • Competition: Incorrect prize notifications
  • Cost trap: Expensive premium rate numbers
  • Fraud: Fraudulent calls (e.g. grandchild trick)
  • Ping call: Short ring to provoke a callback
  • Survey: Unsolicited surveys
  • Other: Other types of SPAM

Mark number as legitimate

If a number has been incorrectly marked as SPAM, you can rate it as legitimate. This helps to correct incorrect messages and improve the quality of the database.

Mark as legitimate

Tap on "Mark as legitimate" to give a positive rating. Optionally, you can also add a comment here.

When should I do this?

Mark a number as legitimate if:

  • It is a serious call
  • The number comes from a real company
  • You know the number and it was reported incorrectly
  • The call was requested (e.g. doctor's surgery, tradesman, parcel service)

Note: Legitimate ratings help to remove the number from the SPAM database or lower the SPAM score.

Thank you for your help!

Every report helps to keep the PhoneBlock database up-to-date and reliable. The community benefits from your experience.
