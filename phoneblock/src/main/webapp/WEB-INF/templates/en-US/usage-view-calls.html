View blocked and missed calls

PhoneBlock Mobile checks all incoming calls from unknown numbers and shows you which have been blocked and which have gotten through.

Important: Only unknown numbers are checked!

Calls from numbers in your contact list are never checked and do not appear in the PhoneBlock call list. The app only processes numbers that are not stored in your phone book.

Blocked SPAM calls

Blocked SPAM calls are displayed with a red symbol and SPAM score. You will receive a notification about the block.

Which numbers are blocked as SPAM?

SPAM numbers (will be blocked)

A number is blocked as SPAM if:

  • you have a high SPAM score in the PhoneBlock database
  • The SPAM score exceeds your set threshold value
  • She is on your personal blacklist (regardless of the score)

Result: Your phone does not ring, the call is automatically rejected.

Legitimate numbers (are allowed through)

A number is considered legitimate if:

  • it is not available in the PhoneBlock database
  • It has a low SPAM score below your threshold
  • She is on your personal whitelist (will never be blocked)

Result: Your phone rings normally, the call goes through.

How does the blocking work?

PhoneBlock Mobile uses Android's CallScreeningService to screen incoming calls before they ring. If the number is in the SPAM database and your set threshold is exceeded, the call is automatically blocked.

When should you report a number?

  • The number is not yet in the PhoneBlock database (low or no SPAM score)
  • The call was clearly unwanted (advertising, fraud, etc.)
  • You want to warn the community about this number
  • The number is to be blocked automatically in future

What happens when a message is received?

  • The number is automatically added to your blacklist</x1
  • You will be protected from future calls from this number
  • Your message contributes to the PhoneBlock database
  • Other users benefit from your rating

Important:

If you report a number as SPAM, it will be added to your personal blacklist and will always be blocked in the future. This decision takes precedence over the community rating.
