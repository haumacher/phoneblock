View blocked and missed calls

PhoneBlock Mobile shows you all blocked SPAM calls and missed calls from unknown numbers.

Data protection: Calls from numbers in your contact list are not recorded. The app only processes unknown numbers.

How it works

Automatic blocking

Blocked SPAM calls are automatically displayed in the app. You will receive a notification with the number and the SPAM score.

Call details

Tap on an entry in the call history to see details:

  • SPAM score: Number of negative ratings from the community
  • Time: When the call was made
  • Categories: Type of SPAM (e.g. advertising, fraud, competition)
  • Comments: Reports and experiences of other PhoneBlock users

From here you can also rate the number yourself or add it to your personal blacklist/whitelist.

How does the blocking work?

PhoneBlock Mobile uses Android's CallScreeningService to screen incoming calls before they ring. If the number is in the SPAM database and your set threshold is exceeded, the call is automatically blocked.
