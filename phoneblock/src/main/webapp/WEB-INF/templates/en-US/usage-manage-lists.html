Manage personal blacklist and whitelist

In addition to the community database, you can keep your own lists to customize call filtering.

What are blacklist and whitelist?

Blacklist

Numbers on your blacklist are always blocked, regardless of whether they are known as SPAM in the PhoneBlock database or not.

Use: For annoying callers that you want to block personally.

Whitelist

Numbers on your whitelist will never be blocked, even if they are reported as SPAM in the PhoneBlock database.

Use: For important numbers that could be blocked by mistake.

Step 3: Display list

Your personal numbers are displayed

Add number

To add a new number to your blacklist or whitelist, tap on the plus symbol (+) at the top right of the respective list. Then enter the phone number (with country code for best results).

Remove number from list

Swipe left over the number and tap the trash can icon to remove it

Synchronization:

Your personal lists are synchronized with your PhoneBlock account and are automatically available on all your devices on which you are logged in with the same account.

Tips for use

Blacklist

  • Add numbers that bother you personally
  • Even if they are not yet in the PhoneBlock database
  • The number is blocked immediately
  • Ideal for persistent callers

Whitelist

  • Add important numbers (doctor, school, work)
  • Prevents accidental blocking
  • Has priority over the community database
  • Particularly important for critical calls

Note: Your personal lists always take precedence over the PhoneBlock community database. A number on your whitelist will never be blocked, even if it has been reported as SPAM by thousands of other users.
