Choose your platform

FRITZ!Box

Recommended: Install the PhoneBlock answering machine as a VoIP device. The answering machine automatically accepts spam calls and engages callers in pointless conversations - your phone won't even ring.

Alternative: Import the blacklist as Phonebook in your FRITZ!Box from AVM. Callers are rejected directly (note: phone book size is limited, therefore not all numbers are recorded).

Android

Block spam calls directly on your Android cell phone with the PhoneBlock app. The app checks incoming calls in real time and automatically blocks known spam numbers. You can set yourself how many negative messages a number should be blocked for.

Features: Call history, report and rate numbers, own block list, no authorization required to read your contacts.

iPhone

Include the PhoneBlock blocklist in your iPhone contacts. iOS then automatically shows you when a caller is on the blocklist. The setup is done via CardDAV - your iPhone automatically keeps the list up to date.

Telephone system

Integrate PhoneBlock into your phone system or PBX. The blocklist can be downloaded in various formats or queried via API. Ideal for companies and professional telephone systems.

The community makes the difference

Strong together:

Anyone can join in! Report spam numbers directly in the app, on the website or by blocking in your FRITZ!Box. As soon as a number is reported, it is added to the block list for all users. The more users participate, the better we can all protect ourselves from spam calls.
